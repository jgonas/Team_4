Adventure Sports

Hang Gliding

Hang gliding delivers an exhilarating blend of peace and thrill, offering the unique opportunity to soar like a bird. This adventure allows you to glide silently over stunning landscapes, providing a profound sense of freedom. It's an ideal experience for those eager to embrace the sky's vastness and enjoy the world from a majestic aerial view. Perfect for both novices and seasoned gliders, hang gliding is a journey unlike any other, promising moments of serene beauty high above the ground.

Skydiving

Skydiving delivers an instant adrenaline rush, combining thrilling freefall with a serene parachute glide. As you jump from the plane, you experience pure exhilaration, followed by peaceful views of the earth below. Perfect for thrill-seekers, it's an unforgettable leap into freedom and excitement.

Hang Gliding and Skydiving Combo

The Skydiving/Hang Gliding Combo Experience offers thrill-seekers an unparalleled adventure that combines the exhilarating free fall of skydiving with the serene, soaring sensation of hang gliding. This unique package is designed for those who crave the ultimate in aerial adventure, allowing participants to experience the best of both worlds in one unforgettable day. Please note, due to the advanced nature of this combo experience, participants must have prior experience in both skydiving and hang gliding to ensure safety and maximize enjoyment. Prepare to embark on a journey that will take you to new heights, testing your skills and daring like never before.

Participants for parachuting need to be 18+ and participants of hang gliding need to be 16+.

Prices are dependent per party, and we can send you an estimate ASAP once you email us an order form.

Before You Book

  1. Valid identification (ID or passport).
  2. Medical clearance certificate, if required.
  3. Signed order form.
  4. Confirmation of minimum age requirement met (16+ for hang gliding, 18+ for skydiving).
  5. Payment method (credit card, bank transfer, etc.).
