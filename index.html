Company Story and History

Welcome to Harry and Harriet’s Hang-gliding Haven! We started our journey in the thrilling world of hang-gliding and skydiving back in 2005. What began as a passion for adventure sports soon turned into a full-fledged business dedicated to providing top-notch products and services to enthusiasts all over the world. Over the years, we have grown from a small garage operation to a renowned brand known for quality, safety, and exceptional customer service. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of countless thrill-seekers, and we continue to push the boundaries of innovation in the industry. We took over "Harry and Harriet’s Hang-gliding Haven" in 2005 and retained the name in a sign of respect to what Harry and Harriet meant to the industry. Harry and Harriet were the two forces that made skydiving as we know it today, contributing significantly to the modernization and accessibility of skydiving for enthusiasts worldwide. Looking ahead, we are committed to pioneering advancements in hang-gliding technology, expanding our reach to bring the joy of soaring to new heights, and nurturing the spirit of adventure that Harry and Harriet so boldly exemplified. Our eyes are set on the skies, but our mission is grounded in a deep-seated respect for the sport, its enthusiasts, and the dreams that lift us all.
