Harry and Harriet’s Hang-gliding Haven

Company Story and History

Welcome to Harry and Harriet’s Hang-gliding Haven! We started our journey in the thrilling world of hang-gliding and skydiving back in 2005. What began as a passion for adventure sports soon turned into a full-fledged business dedicated to providing top-notch products and services to enthusiasts all over the world.

Over the years, we have grown from a small garage operation to a renowned brand known for quality, safety, and exceptional customer service. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of countless thrill-seekers, and we continue to push the boundaries of innovation in the industry.
