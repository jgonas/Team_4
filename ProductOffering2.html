Our products include a broad range of items that will not only be beneficial and necessary for you in your hang gliding or sky diving journey, but will give you the opportunity to take something home to remember the amazing experience you created! Below is a list of our products, starting off the with various equipment packages and products, and then moving on to the souvenirs and merchandise which include t-shirts, key chains, and the photography package.

Hang Gliding Equipment Full Package $799.99

This equipment package includes all the necessary items needed to embark on your hang gliding journey. This is intended for self-guided hang-gliding adventure. For guided hang-gliding adventures, equipment will be included in the cost, and more details can be found on our Company Services Page. The package includes the hangliding wings, harness, control bar, rigging equipment, reserve parachute, helmet, and navigation/safety instruments.

Sky Diving Equipment Package $899.99

This equipment package includes all of the necessary items needed to embark on your sky diving adventures. If you are looking for a guided sky-diving adventure, where equipment will be included in the total cost, please see our Company Services Page. This equipment package includes goggles, a helmet, jumpsuit, harness, and parachute system.
